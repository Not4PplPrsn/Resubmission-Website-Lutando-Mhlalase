Who we are

The Mary Seacole Nursing School is Cambridge approved nursing college, founded by the woman of the same name and based on her legacy of curiosity, understanding and healing. This school was founded during the Crimean war where medical giant like Florence Nightningale, who were innovating and creating new practices that would revolutionize the medical field, of which Mrs Seacole was not in any way infurrior to them in any way. Inspite of the adversity of the that time she manage to fulfill all she wished to, and founded this school, one of the only school with in the empire and later the common wealth which taught people of color in the medical arts. This was for her time and today a remarkable woman.
